Joshua Yang is a freelance journalist writing about international affairs. His work appears in The Washington Post, Foreign Policy, Rest of World, LA Review of Books, and elsewhere. Joshua has covered the exploitation of Indian laborers in Israel, the Trump administration’s broken promises for Afghans, the pro-democracy movement in Hong Kong, and Armenians in East Jerusalem, among other topics.

Joshua is a master’s student at the University of Cambridge as a Gates Cambridge scholar. He graduated magna cum laude from Princeton University with a degree in philosophy.
